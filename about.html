About Eve Daughters

Our story and commitment to beauty

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Eve Daughters Salon has been serving the Kumi community with premium beauty services. What started as a small braiding shop has grown into a full-service salon offering a wide range of beauty treatments.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone deserves to look and feel their best. Our mission is to provide high-quality beauty services in a welcoming environment where clients can relax and enjoy their transformation.

Our Team

Our team of certified beauty professionals is dedicated to continuous learning and staying updated with the latest trends and techniques in the beauty industry.
